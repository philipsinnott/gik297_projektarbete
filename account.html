About

Elon Musk was born in Pretoria, South Africa. When he was 12, he programmed a game called Blastar which he sold to a newspaper. He studied at the Queens University and University of Pennsylvania where he got a bachelor's degree in physics and economics. He later moved to California and studied at Stanford University but decided to drop out to pursue being an entrepreneur. He created the company Zip2 which he later sold for 34 million dollars. He then, along with partners created a company called X.com which in 2001 changed its name to Paypal. He later created Space Exploration Technologies or SpaceX for short. He is also active in Tesla Motors, SolarCity, Neuralink, OpenAI, The Boring Company and Hyperloop. He is currently the fourth richest person in the world with a networth of approximately 153.3 billion dollars. He is most known for Tesla Motors, Paypal and SpaceX. And has a goal of being able to send a manned and unmanned mission to Mars during the first half of the 2020s. As well as being able to create fully self-driving electric cars.
